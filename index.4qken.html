Add an Image to a Post

You can add images to your post via the + icon on the post editor. Click the + and select them, then add them to your post. If you have more than one awesome image to show off, go ahead, you can share multiple ones at once.

Share Content from the Web

With the Chrome extension installed, you can easily share content from anywhere on the web to The Grid.

It's easy! Just navigate to something awesome to share. Right click, then select Share. This will create a new post with the content in your drafts dashboard.

Move Up / Move Down

The Move Up and Move Down buttons allow you to place the image above or below text within your blog post.

  • First, click on your image
  • Then, simply click either button to move the image to where you would like it placed.
